# MediaCrawler Platforms

## Supported Platforms

### Xiaohongshu (Little Red Book) - `xhs`
- **Search Mode**: Search notes by keywords
- **Detail Mode**: Extract details of specific notes
- **Creator Mode**: Extract notes from a creator's homepage
- **Login Types**: QR Code, Phone, Cookie

### Douyin (TikTok China) - `dy`
- **Search Mode**: Search videos by keywords
- **Detail Mode**: Extract video details
- **Creator Mode**: Extract videos from a creator's homepage
- **Login Types**: QR Code, Cookie

### Others
- Bilibili (`bili`)
- Kuaishou (`ks`)
- Weibo (`wb`)
- Baidu Tieba (`tieba`)
- Zhihu (`zhihu`)

## Storage Options
- `json`: Standard JSON output
- `csv`: CSV spreadsheet
- `excel`: Excel spreadsheet
- `db`: Database (MySQL, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, SQLite)
